FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

How does the 14-day trial work? ▼

Start your trial instantly — no credit card required. You get full access to all Pro features for 14 days. At the end of your trial, choose a plan or continue on our free tier.

Can I import from Jira or Asana? ▼

Yes! We have one-click importers for Jira, Asana, Trello, Linear, and Monday.com. Your data, issues, and history are migrated automatically in minutes.

Is my data secure? ▼

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Enterprise plans include dedicated infrastructure.

What integrations are available? ▼

We integrate with 50+ tools including GitHub, GitLab, Slack, Figma, VS Code, Discord, Notion, and more. Our API and webhooks let you build custom integrations too.
